chicken roll

A Chicken Roll is a flavorful wrap made with marinated chicken, fresh veggies, and sauces, all rolled in a soft flatbread, perfect for a quick and tasty snack or meal.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • FLatbread
  • Onions
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato

Steps

  1. Marinate
  2. Cook chicken
  3. Prepare Flatbread
  4. Assemble together
  5. Roll and serve
