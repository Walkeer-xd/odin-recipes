Biryani

hyderabadi biryani

Hyderabadi Biryani is a fragrant and spicy rice dish made with basmati rice, marinated meat, and aromatic spices, known for its rich flavor and cultural heritage from Hyderabad, India.

ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Chicken
  • Yogurt
  • Onion
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Marinate
  2. Fry Onions
  3. Cook Rice
  4. Layer ingredients
  5. Dum Cook
