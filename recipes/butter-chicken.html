Spicy Butter Chicken

Butter Chicken is a creamy, rich curry made with marinated chicken cooked in a smooth, spiced tomato-based gravy, offering a perfect blend of flavors.Butter Chicken is a creamy, rich curry made with marinated chicken cooked in a smooth, spiced tomato-based gravy, offering a perfect blend of flavors.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Butter
  • Onions
  • Cream
  • Tomato

Steps

  1. Marinate
  2. Cook chicken
  3. Prepare gravy
  4. Combine gravy and chicken
  5. Simmer and serve
